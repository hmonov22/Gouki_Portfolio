ABOUT

Hello, I'm Gouki. For me, design isn't just about making things look good; it's about making them feel right. I'm a Junior UI/UX Designer driven by a passion for understanding user needs and translating them into meaningful and clean digital experiences.

I believe the best designs come from a place of empathy. My process often starts with user research, moves through sketching and wireframing, and comes to life in interactive prototypes in Figma. I enjoy every step of turning a complex problem into a simple, elegant solution.

As a designer, I'm constantly learning and exploring new ways to create seamless user journeys. I'm excited to bring my skills and passion to a team that values clean design and user-centered thinking.
